In my second decade after the affliction, Master Edmonstone's castle became my castle. To those in town, rumors spread that Lottie Edmonstone became a recluse. Never leaving. Never socializing. She used to throw such extravagant banquets, they would say. She served her town well, they would say. Protected the town. In reality, she and everyone else in the castle served me.
