And it was the Barrs that got me out of that g/ambling hell
They jammed the lever just then, mid squeeze
A sudden reminder that I know fuck about love

It's become so hard to... ask
Conversation's calcified--
the bones of close talk creaky... fragile--
Can we let go of the models of us 
for long enough
to be who we already are
To end the night with tickets crumpled in our pockets again
under the weight of our empty wallets
happy, though.
