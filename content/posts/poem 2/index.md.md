two hundred fifty years ago smart scientists
so i'm told
put chemicals together that exploded
and keep exploding over and over
wow
it's the best explosion that has ever happened
or ever will happen.
but i can't seem to avoid scooping my paddle
into chunks of debris
while rowing gently past in my canoe
getting tanned from the southern heat
unable to hear the ducks
